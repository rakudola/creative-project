Who We Are

Where Will You Go Today?

Books can take you anywhere. 5354 aims to bring that experience to your devices, letting you put yourself in the protagonist's shoes with an interactive choose-your-own-adventure experience.

The Process

Our writers bring the 5354 experience to you through a combination of traditional narrative writing skills and web programming. After drafting, writing, and editing the stories, we make the stories dynamic with Javascript.

The People

Our main writers are LY and SJ. LY is the programmer, designer, and main writer. SJ is our co-author and cheerleader.
