Have you ever wished you could be the protagonist of a story that wasn't a One Direction fanfiction? Now you can! 5354 is a collection of stories that, using Javascript, allows you to put yourself into the story.

Intro

You find yourself standing in a library. Rows upon rows of books stand before you, lined up like trees in an orchard. It's a book-lover's dream, you think. Or a college student's worst nightmare.

The ceilings soar above your head, sunlight filtering in from a place you can't fathom. You don't see a soul in sight. How did you get here? A journey gone awry? A necessary pit stop? Another assignment by a sad CS student you have to grade?

It doesn't matter. What matters is that you're right here, right now, and there are a lot of books in front of you.

You step forward and pick out a book.
